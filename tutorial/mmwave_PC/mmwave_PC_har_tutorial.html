Dataset with radHAR:¶

radHAR dataset is designed to use mmWave PC data collected by IWR1443Ti to predict the actions of the users. There are totally 5 actions in the dataset: [‘boxing’,’jack’,’jump’,’squats’,’walk’] In the library, we provide a dataloader to use mmWave PC data , converted into voxel image, and predict these actions.

Create model¶

In[4]:

model = har_MLP(dataset="radHAR", num_classes=5)
print(model)
